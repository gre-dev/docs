Once you become an affiliate partner at Greip, you should setup your payout settings so we can transfer your earnings as soon as you reach the account threshold. Let's dive into this:

<Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        Go to the [Affiliate Partner Dashboard](https://dashboard.greip.io/affiliates) of your account
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        Click on the 'Payouts' button

        <Frame caption="Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Accessing payouts section">
            ![Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Accessing payouts section](/images/dashboard-affiliate-payouts.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Third Step">
        Click on the 'Payouts settings' button to manage the settings

        <Frame caption="Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Accessing payout settings">
            ![Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Accessing payout settings](/images/dashboard-affiliate-settings.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Forth Step">
        Then you will need to provide the necessary information by filling in the fields.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Fifth Step">
        Once you complete filling in the required fields, click on the 'Save changes' button, and you're ready to start promoting greip and earn commission rates ðŸŽ‰
    </Step>

</Steps>
