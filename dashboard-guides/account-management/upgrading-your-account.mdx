In order to renew/upgrade your plan you need to follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to the [API section](https://greip.io/dashboard/APIs) from the account
    dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Under the 'Fraud Prevention API' section, click on the Renew button as shown
    below:
    <Frame caption="Greip Dashboard - Upgrading the account">
      ![Greip Dashboard - Upgrading the account](/images/dashboard-upgrade.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Then, from the next page, choose your plan & period then click 'Checkout' to
    complete the payment process.
  </Step>
</Steps>
