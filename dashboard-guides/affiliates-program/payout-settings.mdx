Once you become an affiliate partner at Greip, you should setup your payout settings so we can transfer your earnings as soon as you reach the account threshold. Let's dive into this:

<Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        Go to the [Affiliate Partner Dashboard](https://greip.io/dashboard/affiliates) of your account
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        You should see a warning message at the top of the page, click on the 'complete your payout settings' button

        <Frame caption="Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Accessing payout settings">
            ![Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Accessing payout settings](/images/dashboard-affiliate-payouts.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Third Step">
        Once you access the 'Payouts Settings' page, you will need to provide the necessary information by filling in the fields. The page should appear as follows:

        <Frame caption="Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Complete payout settings">
            ![Greip Affiliates Partner Dashboard - Complete payout settings](/images/dashobard-affiliate-payouts-settings.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Forth Step">
        Once you complete filling in the required fields, click on the 'Save changes' button, and you're ready to start promoting greip and earn commission rates ðŸŽ‰
    </Step>

</Steps>
